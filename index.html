Dummies

Soaryn (www.twitter.com/Soaryn117 && www.twitch.tv/soaryn) said in his October 9th livestream that he wished he could shoot clones at his brother, XCompwiz. This is a mod that allows you to do exactly that! The "**Clone Launcher" item is located in the misc tab of the creative menu. You can also craft it using a bow, a diamond, and a bed. The item works exactly like a vanilla bow, except it shoots short lived copies of you.
